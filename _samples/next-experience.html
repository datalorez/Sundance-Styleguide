About NEXT FEST

Sundance NEXT FEST brings the summer's boldest indie movies and music to the heart of LA. Straight from the Sundance Film Festival in Park City, these movies make their LA debut.

“Sundance Next Fest is the film and music anti-festival you've been dreaming of.” —Noisey

“Sundance NEXT FEST … features the kind of down and dirty films we love.” —Vice

Ace Hotel Downtown Los Angeles

A delicately restored, 1,600-seat movie palace from the 1920s with a three-story grand lobby, an ornate open balcony and mezzanine, and a vaulted ceiling with thousands of tiny mirrors that glimmer when lit—the Theatre at Ace Hotel is one of the most remarkable gems in the city.

929 S. Broadway, Los Angeles, CA
