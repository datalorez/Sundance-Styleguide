NEXT FEST After Dark

A summer celebration benefiting Sundance Institute and its artists

Thursday, August 10
7:30 p.m. Screening
9:30 p.m. Party
The Theatre at Ace Hotel, 929 S. Broadway, Los Angeles, CA

Get Tickets More Info Watch Trailer

Join us for our opening night celebration at Sundance NEXT FEST, a summer festival where movies, music, and mischief collide. The evening will kick off the festival with a special 35mm screening of Quentin Tarantino’s cult classic Reservoir Dogs, followed by an outdoor party with music, drinks, food trucks, games, and more—all while benefiting Sundance Institute and the artists we support year-round.

Presented by Acura.
